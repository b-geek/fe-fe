Our Services

Our bodies change over time, whether due to aging, shifts in weight or pregnancy. Sometimes, we embrace those changes. Other times, they may create roadblocks to our mental well being. The same goes for body issues we were born with and see as undesirable.

No matter the reason you want to redefine yourself, our plastic surgeons are here for you. Among the most trusted names in plastic surgery and leaders in their field, they perform more than 1,000 body sculpting surgeries every year. They work with patients to develop a natural vision for their future self, and they follow up after surgery to make sure each patient’s individualized recovery program is working toward optimal healing.

Cryolipolysis

Cryolipolysis simply involves destroying the fat cells by freezing. It’s based on the principle that adipocytes are susceptible to cold temperatures than other cells. This body contouring procedure is popularly known as Coolsculpting.
