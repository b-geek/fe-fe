Laser Therapy

Low-Level Laser Therapy

This procedure uses cold laser energy. It works by changing the expression of the hormone that regulates your appetite. The therapy also induces changes in your adipocyte metabolism.

The devices for this procedure usually have a light wavelength of 532 nm to 635 nm. When they emit the energy into your body, the fat cells absorb it. This leads to the breakdown of the cells, which are then absorbed in the body.

A good example is SculpSure. It’s the first FDA-approved non surgical body contouring procedure for inner thighs, flanks, outer thighs, abdomen, and back. It usually takes 25 minutes per session. You’ll need several sessions to ensure maximum results.

High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound

Ultrasound is a recent procedure for nonsurgical body contouring. It involves the use of focused sound waves to disrupt fat cells. This causes the cells to dissolve over time.

The treatment works by creating deep areas of necrosis in the subcutaneous layer. This leads to a reduction in the volume of the layer, which later tightens to give you the results. It takes about 12 weeks to notice the results. Again, patients need more than one treatment to tighten and tone the skin.
